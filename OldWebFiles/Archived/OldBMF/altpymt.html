Alternate Payment & Foreign Shipping Information


Many people are still uncomfortable ordering things over the internet. With identity theft, it's certainly understandable. That's why we have both fax and mail orders available for your convenience and safety.


FAX ORDERS
On a single sheet of paper (no cover sheet necessary), please write the number of copies of the book (and/or DVDs), your credit card information (type, number, name on card, expiration, last 3 digits on back), email, and shipping address. Fax to: 512.244.0140. You will receive an email confirmation with your total. **Due to an overwhelming amount of SPAM, we must keep our fax machine on manual mode. If you would like to place an order via fax, please contact us first**


MAIL ORDERS
Please enclose a check or money order $17.00 per DVD (includes shipping), number of copies ordered, and shipping information to: (contact us for address)


Please make checks payable to "Blue Moose Films."


FOREIGN SHIPPING
We know that the foreign shipping charges are outrageous, but unfortunately the price to ship abroad is quite outrageous even through the cheapest means available (United States Postal Service).


ENVIRONMENTAL SUPPORT
Blue Moose is committed to supporting organizations who actively work toward a healthier world. We give 10% of our profits to The Arbor Day Foundation. We use only recycled paper in our daily business operations and we are fervent recyclers of anything that can be recycled. Our featured authors Christine & Ethan Rose will be touring the country in an RV powered by bio-diesel for their 2008 book tour.
