Liberty Bound - Exploring Post 9/11 USA

Liberty Bound takes an entertaining look at America’s ongoing struggle to keep a comfortable balance between democracy, capitalism, and fascism. This is a film about historic events that shape history. It is a film about courage and fear; ignorance and knowledge; propaganda and rhetoric.


So many years since 9/11... what more do we know?
Discover what we knew then and compare to what the country now accepts...
Learn from Howard Zinn's insight about the war in Iraq a month before it started...
Listen to the declassified in flight recording of Flight 93...
Watch a man interrogated on a train for having a philosophical conversation
Remember the totalitarian climate in the country after 9/11
