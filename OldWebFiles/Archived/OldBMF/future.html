Future Plans


At any given time, we have several dreams of what the future might hold for us. At the moment, however, we're dedicating most of our efforts to the creation and promotion of the Rowan of the Wood book series. It will be a total of five novel and at least three graphic novels.


Additionally, we hope to see Rowan on screen, whether that be a feature film or television series is yet to be seen.


Apart from Rowan, we have a few other projects on simmer:


Syndrome - Screenplay Complete, Novel in Development
An Iraqi War Veteran descends into madness due to a debilitating physical and psychological disease. Psych Thriller.


Macbeth - Adapting for Screen
A bloody new twist on an old classic. Horror.


Magdalene - Screenplay under revision
Exploring the Goddess of Christianity, wife of Yeshua bin Joseph, and mother of the Black Goddess Sarah. Historical Fiction/Documentary Hybrid.


Nesting Habits of the Feral Hippie - Screenplay under revision

Hippologist Dr. Ben Close tracks the elusive Feral Hippie to the remote mountain regions of Southern Oregon. Mockumentary.
