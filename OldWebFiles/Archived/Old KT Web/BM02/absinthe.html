Let the Green Fairy Seduce you!

The recipe we used:
  • 30g Wormwood (yuk!)
  • 8.5g Hyssop
  • 6.0g Melissa (Lemon Balm)
  • 30g Anise
  • 25g Fennel Seed (yum - licorice!)
  • 10g star anise
  • 3.2g Coriander.
Put dry herbs in large jar with 800ml of wine spirit. Let steep for a week. Add 600ml filtered water and let macerate for another day. Decant off the liquid by squeezing herbs. Wet with vodka and squeeze again. Still (Ethan made a still with an old pressure cooker and some copper wire. He's amazing!) Color the distillate by adding:
  • 4.2g Mint
  • 1.1g Melissa (Lemon Balm)
  • 3.0g Wormwood
  • 4.2g licorice root
Serve with Sprite or over a sugar cube and cold water. We'll also be making a back-up batch with Vodka and not stilling it at the end. That will be mostly for novelty.
