ETHAN IS 35!
July 9, 2003

We had a BLAST on Ethan's Birthday. His father and brother came up to visit. Tom, his brother, cooked us an amazing breakfast in the morning, then we opened gifts. Ethan was surprised to see he got a canoe, something he's wanted for 20 yrs! We all took his new canoe out on Lake Oroville, and then in Chico Creek where we also used a rope swing (not pictured). It was GREAT FUN, to quote Ethan. On to the Peace & Justice Center for a party with some friends, and then MORE gifts from Scott and Tom (father, brother). And, it is hard to believe, but Ethan even RIPPED OPEN the last package.
