Tour

The authors will be at the 1st Annual Sherwood Forest Faire just west of Austin, TX every weekend from February 27th - April 4th selling their wares! Come on by and get an author signed book, some green man art, or a really cool handcarved magic wand or crossbow!

http://www.sherwoodforestfaire.com

This year, Christine & Ethan are looking for more intimate settings for their weekly book signings. In addition to Independent Bookstores, Libraries, and Schools, the authors would love to visit your home!

Just throw a party on a weekday night (Fridays included) and host the authors. Inspired by this NYT article, all you need to do is get at least 15-20 people to come for your dinner party, game night, or Buffy sing-a-long, and have Christine & Ethan there as special guests! They'll do a reading and discuss their book or life as an author if you'd like as well.

EMAIL CHRISTINE if you'd like to schedule a party or invite them to a school, library, or Independent Bookstore near you!
