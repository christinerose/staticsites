Welcome to our World

Blue Moose Press was created out of the desire to give talented authors and independent alternative to self-publishing and the mounting pile of rejection letters from corporate publishers.

Authors have been findign it increasingly difficult to even geth their work read by conventional publishers. Most publishers won't look at an unpublished author without a solicitation from an agent. Fewer and fewer are taking on unpublished authors at all, even with representation.

We are dedicated to giving talented authors a voice and a chance despite their previously published history or status of representation. Outlets for books have increased over the last decade, and so has the audience who reads. We give the public what they want: fresh talent.
